Topic Question Answer
Trade Does Australia import or export any goods to/from China? Australia both imports and exports goods to/from China.
Trade Why does Australia need to import these goods rather than produce them? Australia imports some goods from China because it may be cheaper to import them than to produce them domestically.
What are the major imports and exports between Australia and China? Major imports from China to Australia include electronics, machinery, furniture and textiles. Major exports from Australia to China include natural resources such as iron ore, coal and gas.
Migration Is there an immigration relationship between Australia and China? Yes, there is a historical and ongoing immigration relationship between Australia and China.
What events in history have triggered migration from China to Australia? Events that have triggered Chinese migration to Australia include the Gold Rush in the 1850s and the White Australia Policy, which led to a significant reduction in Chinese migration until the policy was abolished in 1973.
Tourism Why do Australians visit China? Australians visit China for a variety of reasons, including its rich history and culture, cuisine, and natural beauty.
Why do people from China visit Australia? People from China visit Australia for a variety of reasons, including its natural beauty, diverse culture, and opportunities for education and business.
