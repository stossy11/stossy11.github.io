Interesting Facts about China

China is the world's most populous country with over 1.4 billion people.

The Great Wall of China is the longest wall in the world, stretching over 13,000 miles.

The world's largest radio telescope, the Five-hundred-meter Aperture Spherical Telescope (FAST), is located in China.

Chinese cuisine is one of the most diverse and popular in the world, with a variety of regional specialties.
